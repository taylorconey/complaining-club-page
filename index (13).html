Our Purpose

The purpose of the Complaining Club at Purdue University is to provide students with a light-hearted, judgment-free space to voice frustrations, share humorous grievances, and build community through shared experiences!

Our Mission

Giving voice to the voiceless grumblers — the Campus Complaining Club is dedicated to airing grievances, sharing rants, and finding catharsis through collective complaint.
